# Conclude One - Enterprise Pricing Tiers

## Small Business
- **ARR:** $10,000 - $25,000
- **Support:** Standard email support (48 hr SLA)
- **Features:** Core platform, up to 10 users.

## Mid-Market
- **ARR:** $25,000 - $100,000
- **Support:** Priority support (24 hr SLA), access to training webinars.
- **Features:** Advanced reporting, up to 100 users, single sign-on (SSO).

## Enterprise
- **ARR:** $100,000+
- **Support:** Dedicated CSM, Tier 1 phone support (4 hr SLA).
- **Features:** Unlimited users, custom integrations, dedicated instances.
