# Churn Risk Escalation Playbook

## Triggers
- Customer expresses dissatisfaction with support resolution times.
- Customer threatens to cancel or evaluate competitors.
- Open tickets exceed 3 for high-priority issues (like billing or critical bugs).

## Immediate Actions
1. **Tier 3 Escalation:** Immediately escalate all open technical or billing tickets to Tier 3 support for 24-hour resolution.
2. **Executive Outreach:** Schedule an emergency Executive Business Review (EBR) within 48 hours with a VP or higher.
3. **Proactive Communication:** Send an immediate apology email committing to the resolution timeline.

## Financial Incentives (If required)
- If the deal risk is extremely high, you are authorized to offer a 1-month service credit or a 10% discount on the next renewal to secure the account.
