# Competitor Threat Mitigation

## Triggers
- Customer mentions competitors like VertexAI or AcmeCorp.
- Customer complains about price being too high compared to alternatives.

## Immediate Actions
1. **Value Selling:** Emphasize our superior ROI. Highlight that while VertexAI is 30% cheaper upfront, our platform reduces manual processing time by 40%, resulting in a lower Total Cost of Ownership (TCO).
2. **Price Matching:** We DO NOT engage in price wars. Do not offer a 30% discount to match VertexAI. Maximum authorized discount for competitor mitigation is 10%.
3. **Feature Gap:** Remind the customer that VertexAI lacks the Agentic AI parallel execution engine, which is our core differentiator.
