# Upsell & Expansion Playbook

## Triggers
- Customer expresses high satisfaction with the platform.
- Customer mentions onboarding new teams (e.g., European division, new acquisitions).
- Customer asks for faster onboarding or premium training.

## Immediate Actions
1. **Dedicated CSM Allocation:** Assign a dedicated Customer Success Manager to run specialized bootcamps for the new teams.
2. **Expansion Proposal:** Generate a quote for the additional licenses requested.
3. **Volume Discount:** If they are purchasing 50+ seats, apply the standard 15% volume discount automatically.
